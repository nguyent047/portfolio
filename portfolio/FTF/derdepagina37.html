Emergency Services

In General

There is a large number of hospitals in Amsterdam. Spoedeisende Hulp (First Aid Departments) supply rst aid in case of medical emergencies. The hospital addresses are at the end of this chapter. If you want to visit someone in hospital, call to ask about their visiting hours. If there is a personal question or problem you feel you cannot talk about with anyone you know, you can call the Sensoor Helpline or use the chat and email service (both ways of communication available 24 hours a day). You do not have to give them your name and anything you tell them is strictly con dential. If necessary, Sensoor can refer you to the organisation best suited to help you.
