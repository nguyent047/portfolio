Student Housing

UvA (University of Amsterdam)

If you are studying or going to study at the UvA (Universiteit van Amsterdam), check their website for information: www.uva.nl/housing.

HvA (Amsterdam University of Applied Sciences)

Prospective international students at HvA (Amsterdam University of Applied Sciences) can contact the International Of ce at the location where they will be studying, to apply for student housing. Contact the international of ce from your faculty for more information.

Inholland University of Applied Sciences

Inholland University of Applied Sciences, Amsterdam/Diemen has a limited capacity of student housing and offers short-term accommodation to in-coming foreign students. Go to www.inholland.com, select location ‘Amsterdam/Diemen’ and click on ‘Accommodation’. You can also contact Inholland University at the addresses at the end of this chapter.

SRVU

SRVU Studentenbond has been the student union of VU Amsterdam since 1947. As representative of the interests of all VU students SRVU also offers services to students, wants to help with VU improvements and supports student self-development. SRVU is therefore not only concerned with studies and studying, but builds on the three cornerstones of student representation, service and development.

ASVA

ASVA Student Union represents the interests of students of both UvA and AUAS (HvA). The quality and accessibility of education are their priorities. Being a student means having fun, meeting new people and enjoying education. But as an international you may also have a lot of questions. What about the OV- chipkaart (public transport card), for instance and how do you get a good and cheap bike? ASVA Student Union can help you with questions about rooms, Amsterdam and studying.
