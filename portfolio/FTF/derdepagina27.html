Temporary Accommodation

In General

In case you are not able to find a room before arrival, you may want to stay in a hotel or hostel. Finding daily accommodation should not be dif cult. However at certain times of the year (especially during speci c events), most places may be fully booked. Right before the start of the semester, a lot of students stay in hostels, so act quickly when making a reservation.
