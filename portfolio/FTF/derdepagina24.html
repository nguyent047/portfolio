Private Renting

In General

A starting point for your housing needs would be to check the Facebook page of the International Student Network, where you can get in touch with other students. Some general information can be found at www.huurders.info which has an English version. If you happen to speak Dutch, two other great resources are www.kamers.pagina.nl and www.start12.nl/studentenkamers, the latter offering reviews as well.
