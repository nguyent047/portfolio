Libraries

University libraries

All educational institutes have their own libraries, where you can borrow books using your student ID card or library card. Most libraries also have reading rooms, where you can study or read the books you are not allowed to take out.

Public Libraries

The largest public library is the one at the Oosterdokseiland, near Centraal Station, the OBA (Amsterdam Public Library). This library has a wide selection of foreign newspapers and is a good place to start if you want to learn more about Amsterdam. To get a library card you need to bring valid identi cation papers and a document stating your current address (a recent telephone bill or bank statement will do). You can also ll in the form on their English website to enrol, www.oba.nl/oba/english.html. The OBA organises various events, many with free entry. These include lunch concerts in cooperation with the Conservatorium, city walks and bike rides, talks and reading events (similar to lectures).
