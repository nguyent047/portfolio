Pregnancy

In General

If you think you are pregnant, the rst step could be to buy a pregnancy test at the pharmacy. You should also visit your doctor. He or she can tell you for certain whether you are pregnant or not and can give you relevant information. If you prefer anonymity, or wish to talk things over with somebody who does not know you, go to the GGD. If you do not wish to continue your pregnancy, you can get advice and information at the centre for anti- conception, sexuality and abortion Amsterdam.
