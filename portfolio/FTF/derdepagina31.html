Course materials

In General

Textbooks can be purchased in specialised bookshops, but you can also get most textbooks in online bookshops like bol.com. Here you can nd new and second-hand copies. Bol.com is also a good place for selling textbooks that you no longer need. Athenaeum Boekhandel has bookshops in the city centre and near various university buildings. You can purchase books via www.athenaeum. nl (Dutch only). Scheltema on Rokin, a huge bookshop in the centre, also has a great choice and a good website, www. scheltema.nl. Studystore has bookshops in or near various universities in Amsterdam. They also offer books online. For more information go to www.studystore.nl/ en. You can also visit Studystore to sell textbooks you no longer use.

Photocopying

For photocopying, you can use the machines at your faculty or those in libraries, shops and supermarkets. At commercial copy centres you can also have your papers printed and bound. One of these is Lowie Kopie copy centres, which has four shops where you can print, bind, copy and make your own business cards. You can also send them your work online and pick it up later.
