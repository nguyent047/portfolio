Beaches

Blijburg

Blijburg is the sandy beach of the new island IJburg. It is 250 metres long and forty metres wide. There is a restaurant called Blijburg where you can get drinks, ice cream and all kinds of food. The beach is only open in summer.

Pllek

This urban beach is on the IJ waterfront in Amsterdam Noord. You can get a drink or some food in the beach booth. Live music every Sunday afternoon.

NEMO Science Museum Roof

This place offers an enjoyable summer outings with a lovely view of the old town. It is not really a beach but you can do similar things there, like lounging on brightly coloured deck chairs while enjoying a drink or a meal. Open: Tuesday to Sunday from 10.00 to 17.30 hrs. It is closed during the winter and when the weather is bad.

Strand Zuid

In the RAI area, next to Beatrixpark, you can visit a city beach of 2,000 square metres with deck chairs, an outdoor café and you can even get there by boat if you prefer.

Roest Amsterdam

Roest is a creative city oasis, café, bar and city beach. It is located at the Koud Gas Gebouw at the Oosterburgereiland. This urban oasis offers plenty of variety: drink a nice glass of punch on the beach, stroll through the market in the shed or go dancing in the café.

Amsterdam Plage

Amsterdam Plage is a small city beach at a site called het Stenen Hoofd, located in West and behind Centraal Station. By foot you can follow the IJ water side, a walk that will take fteen minutes. Amsterdam Plage is open 12.00 to 23.00 hrs, until 15 August. Also, every year (in August) the free Open Air Film Festival Pluk de Nacht (Seize the Night) will take place at het Stenen Hoofd. For more information check out www.plukdenacht.nl.

't Twiske

’t Twiske is a recreation and nature area north of Amsterdam. It has several small beaches and also walking and biking routes. During the summer you can buy ice cream and snacks at the main beach.
