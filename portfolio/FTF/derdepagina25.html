Housing Corporations

De Key

De Key Housing Associa- tion offers students reasonably priced furnished rooms in and around Amster- dam. As a Short Stay student you can rent a room for one year at the most. Look for all terms and conditions on the website or ask your International Of ce for information.

DUWO

DUWO also offers short stay accommodation via their website, where you can register and look for rooms online.

Ymere

Ymere offers houses in the Amsterdam Metropolitan Area, for example, in Amsterdam, Alkmaar and Haarlem. It is one of the largest housing associations in the Netherlands.

Woningnet

Another option is checking Woningnet, a housing corporation which offers apartments for rent in various regions. At their website you can choose to search for housing in the Amsterdam region.

Elynx

Elynx is a site for expats living in the Netherlands. If you go to ‘Notices’ in their menu, then click on the ‘Offered: Shared Accommodation’ option, you will nd various announcements.

Housing Anywhere

Housing Anywhere is a housing platform on which students that go on exchange for a few months post their rooms available for renting. Incoming exchange students can respond to their announcements. This website operates on a student-to-student basis, without intermediaries.
