Dutch Language Courses

In General

If you have obtained the necessary preparatory diplomas in your country of origin and are planning to enrol in a Dutch course, you will need a certificate stating that you have sufficient Dutch language skills. Students in exchange programmes do not need this certi cate, but of course some knowledge of the Dutch language makes it easier to enjoy your stay. Your university can tell you about Dutch language courses. Sometimes the institutions offer Dutch language courses, you can contact their websites for information. You can start your Dutch language course on the internet. Go to www.duolingo.com and click on ‘Dutch’. It is a free resource with lessons varying from beginner’s tips to fun vocabulary.
