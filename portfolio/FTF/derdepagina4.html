Dutch Cuisine

In General

Dutch cuisine is not very well known. Meals are often a cosy affair where the entire family sits down together. Typical Dutch foods are:

  • Hollandse Nieuwe, raw herring with or without pickles and chopped onions
  • Snert, a thick pea soup with smoked sausage served with rye bread and bacon, often consumed in wintertime
  • Poffertjes, small sweet pancakes served with butter and powdered sugar
  • Stamppot (hotchpotch) with a vegetable and mashed potatoes
  • Zeeuwse mosselen, fresh and plump mussels, served steaming in a bowl.
